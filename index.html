The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers (2002)

Plot Summary

Recent events have left the Fellowship of the Ring shattered and scattered across Middle Earth. Frodo and Sam continue their quest to bring the One Ring to Mount Doom, aided by the tortured creature, Gollum. Meanwhile, Aragorn, Legolas, and Gimli hunt a band of Uruk-Hai to free the other two hobbits, Merry and Pippin, from their captivity. Gandalf is reborn as Gandalf the White.
